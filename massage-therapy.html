FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long is a typical massage session?

Most sessions run between 60 and 90 minutes, though we offer 30-minute express sessions for sports massage, and 120-minute extended sessions for deep tissue and Swedish. We recommend 60 minutes for your first visit so we have time for a proper consultation.

What should I wear or bring?

Just yourself. We provide everything you need including towels and draping. Wear comfortable clothing that's easy to change in and out of. For sports massage, sports shorts are ideal.

Do I need to have an injury to book?

Not at all. Many of our clients come purely for relaxation, stress relief, or as part of a regular wellness routine. You don't need a specific injury or condition to benefit from massage therapy.

Can you combine different techniques in one session?

Absolutely — and it's one of our strengths. Our therapists are trained across multiple modalities, so a single session might blend deep tissue, myofascial release, trigger point therapy, and more, depending on what your body needs that day.

Is parking available?

Yes — free parking is available at Westway Sports Centre. We're also close to Latimer Road and White City tube stations, making us easy to reach from across West London.
