Sports massage is a specialised form of massage therapy designed specifically for athletes and active individuals. FAQ

Sports massage — your questions answered

Should I book before or after exercise?

Both have merit. Pre-event massage (typically lighter and shorter) prepares muscles and improves circulation before activity. Post-event massage aids recovery and reduces soreness. For regular maintenance, we'd recommend 48–72 hours after intense training so your body has begun its natural recovery process.

How often should I get a sports massage?

For regular training, monthly sessions are a good baseline. During heavy training blocks or race preparation, fortnightly or even weekly sessions can be beneficial. Your therapist will advise you on the right frequency for your training load and goals.

What should I wear?

Sports shorts are ideal for lower body work. For upper body treatment, you'll be undraped in the areas being treated. Our therapists are fully professional and trained in client comfort and dignity throughout.

Is sports massage only for professional athletes?

Not at all. Sports massage is for anyone who is physically active — runners, cyclists, gym-goers, weekend footballers, yoga practitioners, and more. If your body is working hard, it deserves proper care.
