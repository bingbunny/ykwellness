About This Treatment

Your natural solution for stress, tension and fatigue

Swedish massage is the most widely recognised form of massage therapy — and for good reason. Using long, flowing effleurage strokes, kneading, friction, and gentle stretching, it works across the whole body to encourage deep relaxation and improved wellbeing.

Perfect for first-time clients or anyone seeking genuine rest, this treatment improves circulation, reduces anxiety, and leaves you feeling refreshed and revitalised. At YK Wellness, our Swedish massage is anything but generic — your therapist tailors the pressure, pace, and focus areas to what you need on the day.

FAQ

Swedish massage — your questions answered

Is Swedish massage suitable if I've never had one before?

It's actually our most recommended treatment for first-timers. The pressure is gentle to medium, the movements are flowing and intuitive, and the whole experience is designed to make you feel comfortable and at ease. Your therapist will check in with you throughout.

How is Swedish different from deep tissue?

Swedish massage uses lighter pressure and longer strokes, focusing on overall relaxation and circulation. Deep tissue uses firmer pressure to target the deeper layers of muscle and address specific areas of chronic tension or injury. If you're unsure which is right for you, contact us and we'll help you decide.

Can I choose the areas I want focused on?

Absolutely. Your consultation at the start of the session is your opportunity to tell your therapist exactly what you'd like — whether that's a focus on the back and shoulders, full body coverage, or avoiding certain areas. We adapt to you.

How often should I have a Swedish massage?

For stress management and general wellbeing, monthly sessions are a wonderful routine. If you're going through a particularly demanding period, fortnightly sessions can make a significant difference. There's really no wrong answer — listen to your body.
